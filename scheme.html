Government Scheme & Subsidiaries

National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA - 2010):

Aims at promoting sustainable agriculture practices.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY - 2016):

Crop insurance scheme to provide financial support to farmers in case of crop failure.

PM-KISAN (2019):

Direct income support of Rs. 6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers.

National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (NREGA - 2005):

While not exclusively an agricultural scheme, NREGA provides employment opportunities in rural areas, including agriculture-related work, to enhance livelihood security.

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY - 2007):

Aims at achieving 4% annual growth in the agriculture sector by strengthening infrastructure and promoting value addition.

National Horticulture Mission (NHM - 2005):

Focuses on horticulture development, including fruits, vegetables, spices, and flowers, through various interventions like technology dissemination and post-harvest management.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY - 2015):

Promotes organic farming by adopting traditional and organic methods, thereby improving soil health and reducing the use of chemical inputs.

Agri-Export Policy (2018):

Aims to double agricultural exports by 2022 by promoting a stable trade policy environment, infrastructure development, and value addition.

MGNREGA - Agriculture Extension (2019):

An extension of the NREGA scheme that includes activities related to agriculture and allied sectors, such as water conservation and land development

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY - 2015):

Aims to enhance the irrigation potential and improve water use efficiency in agriculture.
