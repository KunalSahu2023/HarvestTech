What is HarvestTech ?

As we know that in Bharat, there is One-Third population which is belong to rural area in which they depend on agriculture and but due to unknown of techn. and uneducated they are not able to take benefit of government so they recieve much loss in agricuture.

to full fill Our life. because agriculture gave us food, vegetable & more which is most important in our life. from Bharat, also foreign countries import vegetable in lot of amount because there is no agricuture field in foreign country.

About

HarvestTech is a Agriclture related Webapp which is a service-based website.

HarvestTech helps Bhartiya farmers in farming by using technology to sustanability farming in which they can take much benefits.

Our misson is to help farmers in Agriculture to take full benefits of government's subsidiary & scheme.

In Bharat, Farmers recieve not good amount of their farms (grains, vegetables, ingredients & more).

This is a college-major project in which we have a aim to help Bhatiya Farmers in farming and want to reduce problem of farmers.
